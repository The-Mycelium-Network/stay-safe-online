stay-safe-online

Hardly a week goes by these days without news of a data breach, leaking the private information of millions of users across the globe. Social media companies and big media are tracking users across the web, storing terabytes of personal data to sell to third parties, or fill our timelines lines, and “news” feeds with self-serving Information and fake news.

But how does one make use of the fantastic resource that is the internet, while staying safe, and keeping your private information private? stay-safe-online is a free, growing resource of tools, resources and information that aims to help everyone get the most out of the internet, without sacrificing their security or privacy.
